The movement type used in the watch is listed in the "DETAIlS" section of every product page. All quartz watches require a battery, automatic watches are powered through hand winding or wrist movement. What to do if my battery died within 30 days of receiving?

Most watch batteries can last over 2 years from when they are first put into use. If the watch stops fuctioning, this is most likely due to a prematurely dead battery cell which just needs to be replaced. Any qualified local watch store can replace your battery. Visit Contact Us page or mail us, if your battery died before first 30 days of delivery
